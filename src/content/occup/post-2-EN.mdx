---
title: 'Items Transported by Forklift'
data : 2025-13-01
---

- Pallets ranging in length from 30 cm to 400 cm.  
- Boards and ULD containers (air cargo loading).  
- Metal frames longer than 400 cm.  
- Items sensitive to damage, chemicals, radioactive sources.  
- Baskets/small containers.  
- Steel structures.  
- Industrial machinery.  
- Liquids in 108-liter/205-liter drums and 1,000-liter containers.  
- Paper in large rolls or folded sheets.  
-  Batteries.
